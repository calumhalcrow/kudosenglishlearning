About Kudos

Vision / Mission

Communication is the most vital skill required for delivering messages, opinions and ideas. Without this skill, business and personal relationships are not able initiate and grow. To be able to communicate effectively, fluent and accurate knowledge of English is essential. Thus, it is our mission to cater to the needs and expectations of everyone we teach, and to guide them to mastery of the English language.

Company Profile

Kudos English Learning is a training company that caters for a wide range of services related to the English language. We specialise in providing general English and communication skills courses to corporate clients, and also offer services in document translation, editing and proof reading.

Kudos is capable of bringing a high quality of service to any task undertaken. When it comes to training packages, fully planned courses are taught to your staff in a professional manner. Care is taken with translation and proof reading to ensure that the final documents are written in perfect English and with a style that will hold the attention of the reader.

In our short time in business we have already managed to complete several projects with great success. Working with the Selangor Human Resource Development Centre, we have completed training courses in English skills at several technology and industrial companies nation-wide. In the translation and editing area, we have worked with UPM Raflatac to bring their monthly company newsletter up to the standard you would expect from a Malaysian industry leader.

If you choose to work with Kudos for any of your English language needs, you will be working exclusively with talented people who learned their craft abroad in English speaking countries, or are themselves native speakers. Every project Kudos completes will be done to excellent levels of language quality, and will lift the reputation of your business or organisation to new levels.
