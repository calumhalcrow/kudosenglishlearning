Training Programmes

I encourage the practice of a negotiated syllabus in Kudos’ training programmes. This means providing training based on your organisation’s needs, the trainees’ skill levels and their opinions on what they need to learn.

The following summarises the types of courses Kudos provides to various types of organisations. For detailed course outlines, please get in touch and I'll provide more information.

Corporate:

  • Public Speaking.
  • Presentation Skills.
  • Business Communication.
  • English Communication.

School Children and Students:

  • Phonetics and Stress.
  • English Communication.
  • Course-specific Tutoring (more information below).

General:

  • Translation and Editing.

Course-specific Tutoring

I have also been providing tuition services for students for several years via TuitionByEmi.com. For more information on our tutoring service, please refer to this site.

Course Outcomes

I aim to make course participants:

  • competent and proficient in delivering ideas and opinions.
  • effortlessly fluent and accurate in communication.
  • poised and confident in sharing information and ideas through presentation, public speaking and face-to-face communication.
  • comprehend a good grasp of the English language.
